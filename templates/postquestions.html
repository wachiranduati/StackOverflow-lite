Guide to posting questions

  1. First Search to Ensure that such a quesion was previously answered
  2. Give you question a descriptive title
  3. Write the contents of your question in great detail
  4. Include tags to your question that relate to it directly
  5. Your tags must be comma seperated eg apple, banana, melon
  6. Post the question and wait
